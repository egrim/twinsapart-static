Standing by with empty’s going back

On March 18, 2013 by Sarah With 1 Comments - Photos

With an overnight connection, we slept in the baggage area the night before our standby flight. It had been a long journey so far, and I didn’t make the flight. Teeka was supposed to come back to me in one of these fancy carts. I went down to get her out of doggy purgatory for a little walk before we tried to get on the next flight to Juneau. There was a mistake, a big mistake. Apparently she made it one the flight without me. That’s a big dog travel no-no, but it turned out awesome because Adam was able to pick her up right when she landed, and she was home sweet home!

 

The really funny part was how I had been stressed out about juggling her, going in and out through security. I was fantasizing about persuading the airline staff to send her on without me, but I didn’t say a word. I knew it was against the rules and they couldn’t do it, no matter how logical it was or how nice I asked. I felt like I won the lottery.
